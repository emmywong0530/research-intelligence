# Interaction Specification

## Navigation
The fixed left sidebar changes the visible page without reloading. The active destination uses the mint accent and soft mint background.

## Discovery
All three Discovery views use the same underlying paper records and expose the same fields: title, year, project, paper type, match percentage, relevance explanation, access state, reading time and primary action. Changing view must preserve filters and selection. Table is the default.

## Paper Field
The selected paper remains central and visually elevated. Previous and next controls rotate through the same records. Clicking a card selects it. Review opens the paper view.

## Reading Hub
Time options update the recommended reading-session label. Opening or summarising a paper does not count as completion.

## Research Profile Learning
Suggestions must be visible, explained and reversible through Accept, Modify and Reject controls.

## Settings
Categories switch within a single page. Privacy, keychain and institutional-access rules must remain explicit.

## Modals
Onboarding and institutional access are supporting flows. Escape closes open modals.
